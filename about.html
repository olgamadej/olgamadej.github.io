BIO

I am a passionate web designer and developer with a strong focus on crafting accessible, user-friendly, and aesthetically pleasing websites. With years of experience in front-end and back-end development, I design intuitive interfaces using HTML, CSS, and JavaScript, and enhance user interactivity with modern frameworks like React and Redux. My goal is to create responsive and seamless web experiences that are both functional and visually engaging across all devices.

Additionally, I use Node.js for handling specific backend tasks that require speed, precision, and scalability. Whether working on a small project or a large-scale application, I focus on creating a smooth, intuitive user experience that adheres to best practices in both design and functionality.
